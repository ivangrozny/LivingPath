LivingPath
Generative Font Modification Software


Version 1.0.0
2025 - Ivan Murit

LivingPath is a tool designed to make typographic forms more fluid and constantly evolving. Rather than drawing new shapes, it generates alternatives that allow the characters to adapt to new contexts. In this context, choosing open-source input typefaces is crucial, both legally and because they were originally designed to offer freedom, just like a stream following its own path without restrictions.


Blur layer as a tribute to Neville Brody
Seam carving layer is inspired by Benjamin Dumont
Center line vectors layer made with Raphael Bastide advice
Reaction diffusion layer is still experimental, may crash
